Research Interest

My research interests encompass knowledge graphs (KG), complex networks (protein–protein interaction networks, Yeast, C. elegans neurons, E. coli) and social network analysis (Karate, Dolphins). This includes knowledge graph completion (KGC), link prediction (LP), graph clustering, natural language processing (NLP), and recommendation systems, with a focus on their applications in pattern recognition and complex network analysis. My PhD focuses on knowledge graphs (completion, evaluation, complexity, link prediction), NLP, Transformers, and LLMs.
